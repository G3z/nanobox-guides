Launch Your App

Once your application (app) has been prepared for production you can feel comfortable knowing it will work in production, all you need to do now is hook it up.

This guide is broken down into three sections:

  1. Add a Hosting Provider
  2. Launch your app
  3. Deploy your app

If you don't already have an account with Nanobox, you'll need to create one.

Add a Hosting Provider

Before you can create an app, you'll need to add a hosting provider. Go to your account admin under hosting accounts and add one.

Launch a New App

Once you have a hosting provider, go back to your app dashboard and launch a new app. Nanobox will provision a bunkhouse server on your hosting provider.

Deploy Your App

With an app created, you're ready to deploy your runtime and code. From your app's root directory, run the following:

# login to your Nanobox account
nanobox login

# link your local codebase to your live app
nanobox link app-name

# deploy to production
nanobox deploy
